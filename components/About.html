About Us

In the year 2014, the Murdock's bar and grill opened. Murdock's is located in Loda, IL
where it is greatly appreciated by the community. Murdock's is a great place to unwind, have
a couple drinks, and order some tasty food. With all the love and support, we have recently
decided to add an additional location to further our growth, this new location being in Knoxville, TN.
